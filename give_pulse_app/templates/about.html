About Us

GivePulse is an innovative platform designed to connect blood donors with hospitals and patients who are in urgent need. We aim to save lives by building a direct and reliable bridge between donors and healthcare institutions.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for blood donation across the Arab world and beyond, ensuring no patient has to wait for blood in critical times.

Our Mission

  • Provide faster donations through instant alerts and notifications.
  • Ensure trustworthy donors with verified profiles.
  • Enable transparent communication between hospitals and donors.
  • Maintain a secure database for donation history and case management.

Key Features

Urgent Requests
Hospitals can post real-time blood requests.
Donor Profiles
Track donation history, location, and impact.
Dashboards
Manage cases efficiently.
Real-Time Alerts
Notifications for matching donors instantly.
Secure & Reliable
Encrypted data and verified cases.

Our Impact

GivePulse reduces waiting times for patients, increases awareness about the importance of blood donation, and builds a strong community committed to saving lives.
